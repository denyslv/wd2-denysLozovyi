Introduction

Where analogue soul meets modern indie energy.

Broken Vinyl Club is an indie music collective driven by raw sound, nostalgic aesthetics, and a passion for authentic expression. Blending modern indie energy with the timeless culture of vinyl, the band creates immersive experiences through live performances and exclusive releases.

Whether you are here to discover new music or reconnect with the warmth of analogue sound, Broken Vinyl Club invites you into a world where music is felt, not just heard.
